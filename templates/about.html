About CE

Computer Literacy has become a necessity in the developed and developing countries. CE University of Technology has three decades of experience in offering Computer Science and Engineering programs. The Department of Computer Engineering which currently is the sole center for offering various computer programs, is one of the top ranked departments in the country.

The Department of Computer Engineering was established in 1985 as the first independent computer engineering department in Iran. In 1986, the department officially began admitting undergraduate students in two majors, “Software” and “Hardware”. The department’s graduate studies program was initiated by the introduction of two MS majors in 1987, “Software Engineering” and “Computer Architecture”; the program was later expanded through the introduction of the PhD program in 1997, and the addition of the “Artificial Intelligence” MS major in 1998. In 2002, the undergraduate and graduate programs were further complemented by “Information Technology Engineering” majors at the BS and MS levels.
